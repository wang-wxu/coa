计算机组成与结构

Overview
This is a basic course on Computer Organization and Architecture . This course is meant for Engineering Students of Computer Science and IT, Electronics & Communication, Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering. The Complete Course is divided into Five Sections, you'll learn all the basic to advanced concepts like functional blocks of computer, microprogrammed control, computer architecture, instruction design, pipelining, and format etc.

本课程是一门关于计算机组织与架构的基础课程，适用于计算机科学与信息技术、 电子与通信、物联网、人工智能和软件工程专业的工程类学生。 整个课程分为五个部分，您将学习到从基础到高级的所有概念， 如计算机功能块、微程序控制、计算机体系结构、指令设计、流水线和格式等。
